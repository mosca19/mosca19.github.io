SGM@BiCi: Search Goes Mobile

Program

The meeeting will have one gala session, featuring top executives of leading companies, and four research-oriented, technical sessions according to the following rough schedule.
  • Monday April 28
    • Morning: technical session
    • Afternoon Gala, featuring top executives of leading companies
  • Tuesday April 28
    • Morning: technical session
    • Afternoon: technical session
  • Wednesady April 30
    • Morning: technical session
    • Afternoon: departures
The program is (purposedly) a mixture of things, with technical talks by researchers working in diverse technical fields, intermixed with more business-oriented talks by executives. In this fashion we hope that a coherent picture will emerge, to represent the challenges and opportunities that this exciting field offers for both research and business, and of the relationships existing between the two.
