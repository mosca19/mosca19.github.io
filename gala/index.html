SGM@BiCi: Search Goes Mobile

Agenda for April 28, Afternoon Gala

No one would have believed in the early years of the XXI century that this world was being watched keenly and closely by awesome computing powers; that as people busied themselves about their various concerns they were scrutinised and studied, perhaps almost as narrowly as a person with a microscope might scrutinise the transient creatures that swarm and multiply in a drop of water...

Search and telecom companies have been hitherto two separate worlds, but they are now set on collision course. Will this engender a new War of Worlds?
